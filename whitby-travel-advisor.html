Why Work With a Local Travel Advisor

Booking travel online looks easy until something goes wrong — a cancelled flight, a resort that isn't what the photos promised, a change fee nobody warned you about. A travel advisor is your single point of contact before, during and after your trip, so you always have a real person in your corner.

  • Personal service. One trusted advisor who learns your travel style — no call centres, no bots (unless you want our 24/7 AI assistant).
  • Exclusive rates. Backed by the buying power of TravelOnly, you get access to deals and perks you won't always find online.
  • Full financial protection. As a TICO-certified practice, your payments and bookings are protected under Ontario law.
  • It usually costs you nothing extra. Advisors are compensated by travel suppliers, so you get expert help at the same price you'd pay on your own.
