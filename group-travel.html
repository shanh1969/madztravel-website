How Group Travel Works

  1. Tell us the vision. Destination ideas, rough dates, group size and budget.
  2. We source and negotiate. Resorts and cruise lines quote group rates and perks; we present the best options.
  3. Everyone books individually. Each guest gets their own booking, payment plan and support — no chasing cheques.
  4. We handle the details. Room blocks, flights, transfers, dietary and accessibility needs, and on-trip support.

How many people make a "group"?

For most resorts and cruise lines, a group is around 8–10 travellers or more (roughly 3+ rooms or 5+ cabins), which unlocks group pricing and perks such as complimentary rooms, upgrades or onboard credit. Even if you're just below that, we'll find the best available rate — just ask.

Do you plan destination weddings?

Yes — destination weddings are one of our favourite things to plan. We coordinate the resort, wedding package, guest room blocks, group flights and honeymoon, and act as the single contact for your guests so your inbox stays calm in the run-up to the big day.
