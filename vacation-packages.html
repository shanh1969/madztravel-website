All-Inclusive Vacation Packages, Planned Around You

Sun, sand and zero stress. Madz Travel finds and books the all-inclusive getaway that fits your budget and your bucket list — flights, resort, transfers and protection, all in one place.

An all-inclusive package is the easiest way to travel: one price covers your flights, accommodation, meals, drinks and entertainment, so you can relax the moment you arrive. The hard part is choosing the right resort — and that's exactly where an advisor saves you time, money and disappointment.

What's Usually Included

  • Round-trip flights and airport transfers
  • Accommodation at a resort matched to your travel style
  • All meals, snacks and drinks (often premium options)
  • Pools, beaches, activities and nightly entertainment
  • The option to add excursions, upgrades and travel insurance

Popular Destinations

Punta Cana

The Dominican Republic's beach capital — huge resorts, warm water and unbeatable value year-round.

Cancún & Riviera Maya

Mexico's Caribbean coast: white sand, cenotes, Mayan ruins and resorts for every style.

Jamaica

Montego Bay, Negril and Ocho Rios — laid-back luxury with famous hospitality.

Cuba

Varadero's beaches and Havana's charm, often at the best price of the season.

Why book your package with an advisor?

Two resorts can look identical online and deliver completely different holidays. Madz Travel knows which properties truly are adults-only-quiet or family-friendly-fun, which have the best beaches, and which are worth the upgrade. You also get exclusive pricing through TravelOnly, a real person to call if a flight changes, and full TICO financial protection — usually at no extra cost to you.

When is the best time to book?

For winter sun (December–April), booking 3–6 months ahead usually gets the best combination of price and choice, and many suppliers offer price-drop guarantees. Last-minute deals exist too — tell Madz your dates and budget and we'll watch for the right one.
