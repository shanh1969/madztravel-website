Meet Madz

The traveller behind Madz Travel — and why she's made it her mission to help you explore the world safely and beautifully.

For Madz, travel has always been about more than a passport full of stamps. It's the feeling of standing somewhere new for the very first time — of discovering just how big, beautiful and generous the world can be. That love of exploring, of experiencing places, cultures and people first-hand, is exactly what led her to build Madz Travel.

Over years of journeying around the world, Madz learned that the best trips don't happen by accident — they're planned, carefully and thoughtfully, with an eye on every detail. And along the way she discovered a real gift for the planning itself: mapping out itineraries, timing connections, choosing the right places to stay, and thinking through every part of a trip so nothing is ever left to chance.

Why She Started Madz Travel

She started Madz Travel to share that gift. Her mission is simple — to help others get the same pleasure and benefit from travel that she has, without the stress, the guesswork or the endless research. She genuinely loves sitting down with travellers, understanding what they're dreaming of, and crafting a journey that's safe, seamless and truly their own.

Safe Travel, Every Step of the Way

Above all, Madz cares that her travellers are looked after at every step — that every aspect of the trip is safe, protected and taken care of, so all you have to do is enjoy it. As a TICO-certified advisor affiliated with TravelOnly, that peace of mind is built into everything she books.

And she's far from done exploring. Madz is always learning — new destinations, new experiences, new ways to make a trip special — because she wants you to experience everything she has, and more.

Dream. Explore. Discover. We'll take you there.
